# kalinga-connect

Platform Mission: cognitioplus/kalinga-connect is a dynamic and intuitive online platform designed to provide essential substance abuse services and resources.

Exclusive Focus: The platform's scope is strictly limited to the health, treatment, and rehabilitation dimension of substance abuse.

Legal Compliance: It operates in full alignment with Philippine law, specifically R.A. 9165 (The Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of 2002) and the mandates set by the Dangerous Drugs Board (DDB).
